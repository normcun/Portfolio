About Me

I’m an aspiring web developer with an enthusiasm and drive that knows no bounds.

In 2018, I attained a certificate of completion for a Full Stack Web Development Program at University of California, Irvine because I knew I had a passion and interest for working in the digital world.

Prior to that I attended University of California, San Diego and finished with a Bachelor of Science degree in Physiology and Neuroscience. I worked as the lead physical therapy aide for four years before recognizing that I was drawn to contributing to the platforms of tomorrow.

Outside of web development, you’ll find me at a golf course, a concert, or the kitchen. I won’t be cooking, just eating.
